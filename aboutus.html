Our Story

Kagay-an Quest was founded by a group of passionate travelers who wanted to share the beauty of Cagayan de Oro with the world. Our goal is to provide unique and unforgettable experiences for our guests, showcasing the best of what our city has to offer.

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional travel experiences that exceed our guests' expectations. We strive to create memorable moments, foster meaningful connections, and promote cultural exchange between our guests and the local community.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading travel company in Cagayan de Oro, known for our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and community engagement. We aim to inspire a love for travel and exploration, while promoting the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of our city.
