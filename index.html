Unleash Your Adventurous Spirit in the City of Golden Friendship

Cagayan de Oro, affectionately known as CDO or the "City of Golden Friendship," is a bustling urban center nestled in the Northern Mindanao region of the Philippines. Its moniker underscores the warm and friendly hospitality of its residents, making it a welcoming destination for tourists. The city not only serves as a major economic and educational hub but also as a gateway to a treasure trove of natural wonders and exhilarating activities. From heart-pounding adventures to tranquil escapes, Cagayan de Oro is a versatile destination that has something for every traveler. Fancy taking the plunge into CDO's offerings?
